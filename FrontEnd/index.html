How much does it cost to join LegalConnect?

Joining LegalConnect is completely free! We only charge a small commission (5-15%) on successful case completions. For the first 3 months, we're offering 0% commission to early adopters.

How long does the verification process take?

Our verification team typically processes applications within 24 hours. For urgent cases, we offer expedited verification within 6 hours for a nominal fee of â‚¹500.

Can I practice in multiple cities?

Yes! LegalConnect allows you to practice across India. You can set your service areas, travel preferences, and handle cases remotely or in-person based on your availability.

What happens if a client doesn't pay?

We use an escrow payment system where clients pay upfront. Your payment is guaranteed and released within 24 hours of case completion. We handle all payment disputes.

Do you provide training and support?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive onboarding training, 24/7 support, and regular webinars on platform features. New members also get a dedicated success manager for the first month.

Can I maintain my existing practice?

Yes! LegalConnect is designed to complement your existing practice. You can choose which cases to accept and maintain full control over your schedule and client relationships.
